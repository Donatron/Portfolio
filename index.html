About Me

I am a freelance Web Developer based on the Gold Coast of Australia

With a background in finance, I have spent the past 10 years or so following my other passion of music through both teaching guitar and performing live. In my finance days part of my role was the development of databases and spreadsheets (primarly Access and Excel) for capturing and processing large amounts of information.

In recent years I was approached by a friend to rebuild his businesses website as he was on a limited budget and he felt with my background I could learn what I needed to to get the job done. This experience renewed my passion for all things tech – the part of my finance career I missed the most – and so I embarked on a new chapter to learn Full Stack Web Development

To date I have completed many courses online using Team Treehouse and Free Code Camp and have a solid foundation of skills in:-
